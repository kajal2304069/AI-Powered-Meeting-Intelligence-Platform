# 🚀 MeetMind AI – Meeting Intelligence Platform

## 📌 Overview

MeetMind AI is an AI-powered meeting intelligence platform designed to reduce the time and effort spent on manual meeting documentation, task tracking, and follow-ups in modern organizations.

The platform automatically converts meeting recordings into searchable transcripts, generates concise summaries, extracts action items, assigns tasks, tracks progress, and provides executive-level productivity insights.

---

## 🏢 Industry Problem

Organizations conduct hundreds of meetings every month, but valuable information is often lost due to:

* 📝 Manual note-taking
* ❌ Missed action items
* 👤 Unclear task ownership
* 📉 Lack of accountability
* 🔍 Difficulty searching past discussions
* 📊 Poor visibility into team productivity

Employees spend significant time creating meeting minutes, tracking follow-ups, and searching through old conversations instead of focusing on high-value work.

---

## 💡 Solution

MeetMind AI automates the entire meeting lifecycle:

1. 🎙️ Transcribes meeting recordings using AI
2. 📄 Generates summaries and key decisions
3. ✅ Extracts action items automatically
4. 👥 Assigns tasks to team members
5. 📧 Sends reminders and tracks completion
6. 🔎 Provides AI-powered search across all meetings
7. 📈 Delivers executive analytics and productivity insights

This reduces administrative workload and improves team accountability, collaboration, and productivity.

## 🏗️ System Architecture

```text
Meeting Recording (Zoom/Teams/Google Meet)
                ↓
      AI Transcription (Whisper)
                ↓
       Summary + Decisions
                ↓
      Action Item Extraction
                ↓
      PostgreSQL + pgvector
                ↓
       Task Assignment Engine
                ↓
      Dashboard + Analytics
                ↓
 Email Reminders + Productivity Tracking
```
